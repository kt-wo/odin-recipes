Rum Cake

Enjoy a tropical and boozy treat with this easy-to-make rum cake.

Ingredients

  • 4 whole eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup gold rum, Bicardi
  • 1 (18.25 oz) box classic yellow cake mix
  • 1 (1 oz)small box sugar-free instant vanilla pudding mix

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Grease a Bundt pan
  3. Beat eggs, oil, water and rum in mixer just until incorporated.
  4. Add pudding mix and cake mix and beat on med, about 3 to 5 minutes just until you get a smooth texture.
  5. Pour into pan and bake at 350 degrees F for 40 to 45 minutes.
  6. The cake should have a light golden brown color, but will darken with the glaze
